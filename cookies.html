Cookie Policy

How Picpee uses cookies and similar technologies

This Cookie Policy explains how Picpee uses cookies and similar technologies (collectively, "cookies") when you visit or interact with us on the site.

By continuing to access and use the Site, you agree to our use of cookies as set out in this Policy. You can customize or change your cookie preferences at any time through the "Cookie Settings" section located at the bottom of the home page or by adjusting the settings in your browser (details are set out below).

We may update this policy from time to time without prior notice. Please check back periodically for any changes.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files containing a certain amount of information, which are stored on your browsing device (e.g. computer, mobile phone or tablet) when you visit a website. These cookies can be retrieved by the website that placed them or by third parties using the same type of cookies.

  • First-party cookies: created and used by Picpee.
  • Third-party cookies: placed by Picpee's partners or service providers through the Website.

2. What does Picpee use cookies for?

Cookies play an important role in improving performance and user experience. Specifically, they help:

  • Remember login information, user preferences and language;
  • Support smooth navigation on the Website;
  • Understand how users interact with the Website to improve features;
  • Provide content and advertising tailored to your interests.

3. Types of cookies used by Picpee

Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are required to ensure the proper functioning of the Website, including: maintaining login sessions; accessing secure areas and processing payments and basic service requests.

You cannot disable these cookies through our system. However, you can set your browser to reject or warn you about them. However, this may disrupt some of the Website's features.

Performance cookies

These cookies collect anonymous data about how users interact with the Website (e.g. which pages are visited most often, errors if any, etc.). These cookies help us improve performance and optimize the user experience.

Functionality Cookies

Functionality cookies help remember your personal choices, such as: language, geographic region, font customization, color and size, etc. If you disable this type of cookie, some features may prevent your operations on the website from working smoothly.

Targeting Cookies

These are cookies for advertising and marketing purposes. They:

  • Deliver advertising content that is more relevant to you;
  • Limit the number of times you see an advertisement;
  • Allow us and our partners to measure campaign effectiveness.

The information collected may be shared with advertising partners to display Picpee ads on other platforms you use.

4. How long do cookies stay on your device?

The length of time a cookie stays on your device depends on what type of cookie it is. These include:

  • Session cookies last for the duration of your session and are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies stay on your device for longer and only expire after a certain period of time or when you manually delete them.

5. How to control and manage cookies

You can manage cookies through:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to enable, disable, or delete cookies. Refer to your browser's "Help" or "Settings" section for instructions on how to do this.
  • Cookie Settings on the Website: Click the "Cookie Settings" link at the bottom of the home page to update your preferences.

Note: Disabling some cookies may affect your ability to use the full features of the Picpee Website.

For more information about cookies and how to control them, you can visit www.allaboutcookies.org

Picpee's Customer Support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions regarding the Site or Cookie Policy.
