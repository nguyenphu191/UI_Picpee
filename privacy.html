Privacy Policy

Welcome to Picpee!

This Privacy Policy applies to Picpee (“we”, “us”, “our”) and describes how and why we may collect, store, use, and/or share (“process”) your personal information when you use the services we provide (“Services”), including but not limited to the following:

  • Visiting our website at picpee.com or any page that links to this Privacy Policy;
  • Using services provided through Picpee’s website, mobile applications, or other digital platforms;
  • Interacting with Picpee through online or offline sales, marketing, or events.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you in two ways: (1) information you voluntarily provide, and (2) information collected automatically when you use the Picpee Services.

Personal Information You Voluntarily Provide

We collect personal information that you provide directly when you:

  • Register for an account or use the Services
  • Request support, contact us
  • Participate in promotions, marketing, surveys
  • Or express interest in our products/services.

The types of information we may collect include:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Job title or occupation information
  • User name and password
  • Contact preferences
  • Authentication data
  • Billing address

NOTE: Sensitive information: We do not collect or process any type of sensitive information (e.g., medical data, religion, race, etc.).

Payment Data

When you make a payment through Picpee, we may collect data necessary to process the transaction, such as:

  • Payment Card Number
  • Security Code (CVV)

Information Collected Automatically

Some information is collected automatically when you use our Services. This information does not directly identify you, but helps us improve the performance and security of our systems.

Automatically collected information includes:

  • Access logs and usage data
    • IP address
    • Browser and device type
    • Operating system
    • Pages visited
    • Time and frequency of use
    • Usage behavior such as searches, interactions, button presses, system errors
  • Device information
    • Device name
    • Hardware type
    • Operating system and version
    • Internet or mobile service provider
  • Location data (if permitted)
    • We may determine your approximate location through your IP address, GPS, or information from your browser/device.
    • You can opt out of sharing location data by turning off location access from your device or browser.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Like many online services, we use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Identify devices
  • Analyze user behavior
  • Optimize user experience
  • Improve service speed and stability

Your Commitment: You agree that all personal information you provide to us is true, complete, and accurate, and that you will promptly notify us of any changes.

2. How We Process Your Information

Primary Purposes of Processing Personal Information

  • Create and manage user accounts: To facilitate registration, login, and maintenance of your account on the system.
  • Provide and maintain the Services: To ensure that you can access, use, and benefit from the features, tools, and functionality of the Services.
  • Customer support: Answer questions, process requests, and assist you during your use.
  • Administrative communications: Send notifications regarding your account, services, policies, and other important updates.
  • Perform and manage transactions: Process orders, payments, refunds, exchanges, or related transactions.
  • Enable interactions between users: If the Services allow messaging, sharing, or interactions, we process information to support these functions.
  • Send surveys and collect feedback: To improve the quality of future services and products.
  • Relevant marketing and advertising (if permitted): Send emails, promotional information, or display advertisements that are relevant to your interests and usage behavior. You can opt out of receiving marketing information at any time.
  • Analyze and improve the Service: Evaluate the effectiveness of campaigns, improve performance, and upgrade functionality.
  • Security and fraud prevention: Detect, monitor, and handle fraudulent behavior, violations of terms, or threats to the system.
  • Protect safety: Process information when necessary to protect the identity or safety of users or others.

Processing Data and Files Stored by You at Picpee

In addition to personal information, we may also process files, data, and content you store on our systems for the following purposes:

  • Training artificial intelligence and improving software: Improving the quality of recommendations, automated features, or the overall user experience.
  • Extracting aggregated non-personal data: Such as general location information, demographics, or usage trends for analytics.
  • Creating non-personal marketing content: Using data to suggest more relevant designs, creatives, or content to each user group.
  • Analyzing metadata: Recording device type, file format, resolution, etc. to optimize service compatibility and performance.
  • Providing creative suggestions or content: Suggesting ideas based on drafts or archives you have saved on the platform.

NOTE: Users may refuse to allow the use of data for the above purposes without affecting the quality of the Core Service.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

If your account is canceled or you voluntarily terminate your use of the service, you will have 30 days to download or back up all your data from the Picpee system.

After the above period, we may permanently delete data as part of the system maintenance process, unless the law requires longer retention.

4. Legal Basis for Processing Personal Information

We only process your personal information where we have one or more valid legal bases, in accordance with applicable law. Specifically:

  • Your consent: We process personal data when you actively provide your consent for specific purposes such as marketing, content recommendations, or analysis of service usage behavior. You can withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Performance of a contract: We need to process your information to perform a contract we have entered into (e.g., creating an account, providing a service, processing payments) or to take pre-contractual steps at your request.
  • Compliance with a legal obligation: We may need to process data to comply with the law, such as storing accounting information or responding to a valid request from a regulatory authority.
  • Legitimate Interests: We process data when it is necessary for the legitimate interests of the company, for example: improving user experience, preventing fraud or abusive behavior, analyzing and improving products.

In all cases, we ensure that your privacy and interests are always prioritized and protected.

5. When and With Whom We Share Your Information

We only share your personal information when necessary, in strict compliance with our confidentiality principles. Sharing is always done with qualified parties, with a binding agreement, and for a specific, legitimate purpose.

We may share your personal information with third party service providers, contractors, agents, or partners (collectively, “third parties”) to perform certain tasks on our behalf, such as:

  • Technical support, hosting, and system maintenance
  • Data analysis and performance measurement
  • Providing marketing and sales services
  • Processing payments or verifying accounts

All of these third parties are bound by contract to:

  • Process information only in accordance with our instructions
  • Not to share data again without permission
  • Protect data at a level equal to or greater than our policies
  • Delete or return data after the contract ends or upon request

Examples of third parties include:

  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud computing and data storage services
  • Analytical tools and performance monitoring (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Account authentication and login platforms
  • Payment service providers
  • Product design, communication, and collaboration tools
  • Social media and retargeting platforms

If our company is involved in a merger, acquisition, restructuring, bankruptcy, or transfer of assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction. In all cases, we will require the receiving party to continue to protect the data in accordance with this policy.

Internal affiliates:

We may share information with affiliates within our corporate or group of companies. These affiliates include:

  • Joint venture partners
  • Other entities under common ownership or control with us

Any sharing with an affiliate is subject to the same privacy policy as ours.

6. How Long We Retain Your Information

We only retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services, fulfill our legal obligations, and achieve the purposes set out in this privacy policy.

We will generally not retain personal information for more than 12 months after your account is terminated, unless a longer period is required by law (for example, for tax, accounting, or legal reasons).

When there is no longer a legitimate business need to process your information, we will either:

  • Delete
  • Anonymize, the personal information
  • Securely store and isolate it from further processing, until it can be deleted in accordance with our regular retention procedures.

We are committed to keeping data in compliance with legal requirements, data minimization, and information security.

7. Information from Minors

We are committed to protecting the privacy of minors and do not actively collect personal data from children under the age of 18. We do not target our services or marketing activities to children under the age of 18.

By using the Services, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old, or that you are a parent or legal guardian and have given your consent for a minor under your care to use the Services.

If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under the age of 13, we will immediately delete such data from our systems. If you suspect that we may be holding personal information from a child under the age of 13, please contact us at picpee.com@gmail.com for assistance.

To learn more about how you can control data collection, please refer to our Cookie Policy, which includes detailed instructions on how to opt out of cookies.

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Any third parties we work with are required to ensure that your data is handled securely and confidentially according to their contractual terms. Once we have received the data, we apply strict procedures to prevent unauthorized access. Registered users are also responsible for keeping their password confidential and not sharing it with third parties. If you need more information about our security measures, please contact us directly for support.

10. Your Privacy Rights

In some regions such as the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, Switzerland, and Canada, users have rights under data protection law. These include the right to access, correct, or delete personal data, the right to restrict or object to data processing, the right to data portability (where applicable), and the right not to be affected by automated decision-making. You can exercise these rights by contacting us using the information at the end of this notice.

If you believe that your personal data is being processed unlawfully, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Users in Switzerland can contact the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner.

Where the processing of your data is based on consent, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. This withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of any processing that has already taken place and does not affect processing based on other legal grounds.

If you do not wish to continue receiving marketing or promotional communications from us, you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking on the unsubscribe link in an email, replying “STOP” or “CANCEL” to an SMS message, or by contacting us directly. However, you may still receive communications related to your account or the services you are using.

If you wish to review, update, or terminate your account, you can do so in your account settings. Upon request for account deletion, we will deactivate the deletion of your account and delete the relevant information from our operating systems. However, we will retain some important information for fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

In addition, most web browsers allow you to control the use of cookies. You can refuse or delete cookies in your browser, or use the cookie management tools we provide. However, disabling cookies may affect some features of the Service.

If you have any privacy concerns or would like to exercise your rights, please contact us by email: picpee.com@gmail.com.
